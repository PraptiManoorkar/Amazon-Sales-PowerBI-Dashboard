# Amazon Global Sales Analysis Dashboard | Power BI

## 📊 Project Overview
Interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing 178K+ Amazon sales transactions to identify top performing regions, categories, and shipping modes. Built for business stakeholders to track KPIs and drill down by Market and Category.

## 🛠️ Tools Used
`Power BI` `DAX` `Power Query` `Data Modeling` `Star Schema`

## 📈 Key Insights
- **12.64M Total Sales** and **1.47M Profit** across global markets
- **United States** contributes 27.4% of sales at 2.30M, highest of all countries
- **Technology** is top category with 37.53% of total sales
- **Standard Class** is most used shipping mode by volume

## 🔍 Features
- **KPI Cards**: Total Sales, Profit, Quantity, Avg Shipping Cost
- **Funnel Chart**: Sales ranking by Country 
- **Treemap**: Sales distribution by State
- **Donut Chart**: Market share by APAC, EU, US, etc
- **Slicers**: Dynamic filtering by Market and Product Category
- **DAX Measures**: Profit Margin, YoY Growth, Total Orders

## 📁 Files

1. Download `Amazon_Sales_Dashboard.pbix`
